This is my shpiel.

I'm an engineer.

Yes, a software engineer in the most practical sense, but also an engineer of ideas. I enjoy using the tools at my disposal to imagine and create interesting things. I also enjoy acquiring these tools, and am indeed in a place in my career where there's a lot more learning than using. And I think that's okay.

I'm a father.

This would have been the first section were I to prioritize them based on importance and meaning. There is nothing more integral to me than caring for my daughter.

I'm a teacher.

I have been at various times a classroom teacher and a personal tutor; I love to impart whatever knowledge or wisdom I can to whomever may benefit.

I'm a writer.

There is so much more to writing than haphazardly tossing words onto a medium.


I communicate best through writing. Words yield tremendous power of expression, though inherently imperfect. I believe writing can be the clearest and most thorough way to transmit thought—when done well— and I strive to do it well.


I am also a poet of words and ideas; if engineering is construction using tools, poetry is also, only the rules are whatever they are set to be rather than physical laws.
